Remember, Anything is Popsicle.

Sol Pops are handmade gourmet and wellness popsicles made in Portland, Oregon. Our pops are sweetened with organic evaporated cane juice, agave, or fruit cider, and we intentionally under-sweeten, using just enough to brighten the flavor of the fruits, vegetables, and herbs.

We emphasize local and organic ingredients, and our flavor combinations change with the season.

During the summer, you can find Sol Pops vending from bright blue 1970’s-era cooler trikes under our signature canopy of giant green leaves at these Portland-area farmers markets:

Buckman Farmers Market (Thursdays 3:30–7:30)

Hollywood Farmers Market (Saturdays 8:00–1:00)

Portland Farmers Market @ PSU (Saturdays 8:30–2:00)

King Farmers Market (Sundays 10:00–2:00)

 

You can also find us in places such as New Seasons Markets, ¿Por Que No?,
Hot Lips Pizza, Peoples Coop, and the Food Front Coops.
