Flavors

(aka “I Bet You Two Bucks I Can Make That Kid Stop Crying”)

So far, we have developed close to 40 different flavors. Many of our ideas are born as the seasons change and new local produce becomes available. We make both sweet and savory pops, and also have a line of coconut cream-based pops.

Our all-time best sellers are:

  • Strawberry Lemonade
  • Mango Lime
  • Coconut Agave

Our savory pops include:

  • Cucumber Lime Jalapeño
  • Carrot Lime Ginger
  • Basil Lemon
  • Grapefruit Basil
  • Orange Lavender
  • Mango Lavender
  • Sugar Snap Peas with Orange
  • We even made a “Blueberry Hopsicle” for last years North American Organic Brewer’s Festival, with blueberries and hops tea!

We also make many kid-friendly pops, since children really are the biggest connoisseurs of popsicles and some of our most devoted customers.

Some of our favorite “sweet and tart” pops are:
